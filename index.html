Accelerate communication with push notifications

Push notifications keep your groups updated with the latest information. Messages are broadcast by authorized user’s right from their phone – from anywhere. Your users subscribe to each activity channel they wish to notify.

Real native apps that run on Android, iPhone & tablets

Your organization’s custom app downloads right from Google Play and the iTunes Store ready for use. No clunky web browsers or mobile sites with pinch and zoom. An easy-to-use, clean look your users will love. And you control everything - right from your website admin panel.

Instant auto-updates whenever you make changes

Every time a user launches the application, the app automatically checks for new content updates. Forget costly development and long review periods. You make changes in the admin control panel and your users see them instantly the next time they open the app.
